<p class="lead-highlight">
  This template is designed as a neutral starting point for multilingual sites on GitHub Pages.
</p>

Use this first post to explain what your project is about, who it is for, and where visitors can go next.

<h2 class="custom-heading">Quick Setup Checklist</h2>

<div class="architecture-details">
  <div class="detail-box">
    <h4>Site Identity</h4>
    <p>Update title, description, URL, and contact details in <code>_config.yml</code> and <code>_config_github.yml</code>.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="detail-box">
    <h4>Pages and Navigation</h4>
    <p>Edit pages under <code>_pages/en</code>, <code>_pages/es</code>, and <code>_pages/pt</code> while preserving language references.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="detail-box">
    <h4>Posts and Assets</h4>
    <p>Replace sample posts and images with your own content, links, and media files.</p>
  </div>
</div>
